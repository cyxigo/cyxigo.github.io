Imperfection

I was going through a lot recently, so I made this orchestral track. Why is it called "Imperfection"? Well, it's kind of personal thing to me. Music has always been close to me, it helped me whenever I was sad and when I was happy, it made me even happier. I thought I could never make any piece of music, and when I tried, I've always found it terrible, bad, imperfect. But one day I decided to upload at least something ("A Battle Full of Hopes"). And even if no one was listening to it, I would be proud of it. After finishing "First Works" I decided to master my music making skills, to make an album that would reflect my emotions through the journey of learning how to make decent music - "Imperfection", in honor of my former fear of showing my music.
P.S: Don't worry, this isn't last track in this album ;)
