Midnight

I really wanted to make an Unreachable Midnight sequel, so I came up with this short track, and I'm completely satisfied now. This track too, uses an old motif from my really old .flp (even older than Unreachable Midnight motif!) and motif that you might recognize from start if you listened to my first works ;) (don't want to spoil where it's from, kinda ruines the whole thing) What can I say about track overall: it's calmer than Unreachable Midnight, uses same bpm (180) and same synths. I only added GMS Agressive TE & GMS Lead Classic Vibrato as bass, Sytrus Distant and some closed HiHats from Drumaxx Electro 4 MC because why not, sounds better really. Tell me what you think about this track! Making techno tracks is really fun...
